NEW! Version 1.0.0: Official Launch!

Dec 9, 2025

We are thrilled to announce the official launch of the RCSO Bot bringing a full suite of automated administrative and field tools online Version 1.0.0 includes all core functionality necessary to manage officer status records and training, ensuring smooth and professional operations for the entire department from day one

Automated Shift Management

  • 10-8 / 10-7 Controls Officers use the interactive /shift_panel to clock in and out This function is essential and manages the On Duty role while immediately starting the internal time tracker and logging the start time The system validates the current shift status preventing errors from double clock ins or missing clock outs and ensuring clean data for all personnel
  • Automatic Logging Time is accurately tracked and recorded using the robust internal database Promotion points are calculated based precisely on duty hours worked and are automatically awarded immediately upon clock out (10-7) Officers receive instant feedback on their session duration and points earned aiding in performance management
  • Threaded Logs Every single shift initiated gets a private log thread automatically created for recording activity This thread is accessible only to the officer and authorized command staff Officers can log detailed status changes and major events via /status_update ensuring detailed records without cluttering main channels or losing critical data The thread is locked upon shift end preventing unauthorized modifications and securing the record

Field Reporting Suite

  • Modal Forms The bot utilizes the /report_panel command which triggers instant pop up modals for clear and structured input This comprehensive system supports filing detailed Arrests (Booking) Citations and Incident Reports ensuring all documentation is standardized and complete eliminating typical formatting errors
  • Supervisor Workflow All reports submitted by officers require mandatory supervisor review and approval to become official records Supervisors use interactive buttons (APPROVE/DENY) that appear directly within the reporting thread in the RECORDS channel to finalize the records and complete the workflow Approval of Booking reports automatically triggers a separate log to the JAIL_LOG channel ensuring immediate notification to jail staff

Personnel and Training Tools

  • Disciplinary The system automates disciplinary actions ensuring consistency across the department This includes timed suspension (fixed at 3 days) and discharge actions (24 hour role hold) with instant logging and role management Both major actions come with a vital UNDO feature for immediate correction by authorized command staff reducing the risk of permanent administrative errors
  • FTO Monitoring The bot uses smart voice activity tracking in the dedicated TRAINING_VC The system automatically pauses the training timer if the FTO or trainee mutes or deafens their mic guaranteeing accurate tracking of only active training time Logs are sent to the FTO_LOG channel automatically upon session end providing transparent auditing of training duration
  • Instructor Tools The /training_panel is available for hosts to easily manage sessions announce training and formally log final session results (Pass/Fail/Remedial) for accurate and auditable record keeping Trainees can check their progress via /view_cadet_profile at any time to monitor their status
